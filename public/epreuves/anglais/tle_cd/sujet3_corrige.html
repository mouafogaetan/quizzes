SECTION QUATRE: EXPRESSION ÉCRITE (10 POINTS)

Grille d'évaluation pour la rédaction:
  • Respect du sujet choisi (2 points)
  • Cohérence et structure (introduction, développement, conclusion) (3 points)
  • Qualité de la langue (grammaire, vocabulaire, orthographe) (3 points)
  • Originalité et pertinence des arguments (2 points)
Exemple de plan pour le sujet 1:

"Youth, preservation of peace for an emerging Cameroon"

  1. Introduction: Importance of peace for development
  2. Strategy 1: Promote intercultural dialogue and tolerance
  3. Strategy 2: Encourage youth participation in community peace initiatives
  4. Strategy 3: Education for peace and conflict resolution in schools
  5. Strategy 4: Economic empowerment to reduce youth frustration
  6. Conclusion: Summary and call to action
Exemple de plan pour le sujet 2:

Speech on the importance of Bilingualism

  1. Greetings and introduction
  2. Definition of bilingualism
  3. Advantages for education and career
  4. Role in national unity
  5. Personal benefits
  6. Conclusion and encouragement
Exemple de plan pour le sujet 3:

Town vs Village life

  1. Introduction: State your preference
  2. Argument 1: Better job opportunities (town) / Peaceful environment (village)
  3. Argument 2: Access to services and amenities
  4. Argument 3: Social life and community
  5. Argument 4: Connection to nature / Cultural opportunities
  6. Conclusion: Restate your position
Barème de notation globale:
  • 18-20 points: Excellent
  • 15-17 points: Très bien
  • 12-14 points: Bien
  • 10-11 points: Passable
  • 0-9 points: Insuffisant
