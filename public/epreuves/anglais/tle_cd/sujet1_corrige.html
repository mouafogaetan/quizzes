SECTION D : EXPRESSION ÉCRITE _____/10 points

Critères d'évaluation :

  • Respect de la consigne (2 pts) : Respect de la longueur demandée (220-250 mots) et du sujet choisi
  • Cohérence et structure (2 pts) : Organisation des idées, introduction et conclusion
  • Contenu et pertinence (2 pts) : Qualité des arguments et des exemples
  • Précision linguistique (2 pts) : Correction grammaticale et utilisation appropriée du vocabulaire
  • Variété linguistique (2 pts) : Diversité des structures et du vocabulaire utilisé

Exemple de réponse pour le sujet 1 (Discours) :

Dear community members, young people, and honored guests,

I stand before you today to talk about the importance of valuing all professions in our society, particularly the essential work of sanitation workers. These dedicated individuals play a crucial role in maintaining public health and cleanliness in our community.

Without sanitation workers, our streets would be filled with waste, leading to the spread of diseases and environmental pollution. They work in all weather conditions, often starting their days before sunrise, to ensure our neighborhoods remain clean and safe. Their contribution to public health is immeasurable.

Yet, despite their importance, sanitation workers often don't receive the recognition or respect they deserve. We must change this perception and acknowledge that every profession has dignity and value. A society functions because of the collaboration between all professions - doctors, teachers, farmers, and yes, sanitation workers.

Let us commit to showing respect and gratitude to all workers, regardless of their profession. Let's teach our youth that success isn't just about prestigious jobs but about contributing meaningfully to society in whatever capacity we can.

Thank you.
