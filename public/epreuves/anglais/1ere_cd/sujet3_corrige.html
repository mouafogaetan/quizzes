V. Complete the sentences with the correct answers from brackets (5 marks)

  • 1) may (Asking for permission - "may I try")
  • 2) Although (Contrasting idea - "Although he slipped")
  • 3) since (Referring to time from a point in the past - "since she completed")
  • 4) is being painted (Passive voice, present continuous - "is being painted")
  • 5) where (Referring to a place - "the restaurant where they held")
  • 6) in (Months use "in" - "in March")
  • 7) didn't he (Question tag for past simple positive - "didn't he?")
  • 8) would not have praised (Third conditional - "would not have praised")
  • 9) had listened (Past perfect for unreal past - "I had listened")
  • 10) regularly (Adverb of frequency - "regularly participate")
